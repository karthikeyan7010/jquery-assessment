LED LIGHT



Description

Rab LED lamp in white finish, has plastic construction for added durability. The A19 arbitrary lamp with E26 medium base, has a voltage rating of 120-Volts and current rating of 125-Milli-Amps. The 9.5-Watt lamp delivers soft white light output of 800-Lumens with 2700K color temperature and 80 CRI. It has an average life of 15000 Hours. The lamp provides 230-Degree omni-directional beam angle for even light distribution. The lamp has an efficacy of 84 LPW and power factor of 0.7. It measures 2-3/8-Inch x 4-5/16-Inch. It is suitable for general lighting, commercial and residential applications. It is perfect for damp locations. Lamp meets UL, Energy Star, FCC, RoHS standards.
